How to Play

  1. Objective:

    The goal of the game is to be the first player to reach 100 points.

  2. Starting the Game:

    The game randomly selects which player will go first. Each player takes turns rolling the dice.

  3. Player's Turn:

    On your turn, click the “Roll” button to roll the dice. The number you roll will be added to your current score.

  4. Rolling a 1:

    If you roll a 1, your current score for that turn is reset to 0. Your turn ends, and the next player takes their turn.

  5. Shunk (Hold):

    At any point during your turn, after rolling, you can click the “Shunk” button to "bank" your current score. Your current score is added to your total score, and it becomes the next player's turn.

  6. Winning the Game:

    The first player to reach or exceed 100 points in their total score wins the game!

  7. Game Over:

    Once a player wins, click the “Play Again” button to reset the game and start over.

Important Notes:

  • You must roll the dice at least once before you can "Hold" and save your score.
  • Rolling a 1 will reset your score for that turn to 0, so decide whether to risk rolling again or bank your points!
